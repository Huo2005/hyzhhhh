Favorite Teams:

  • Clippers: Not only because there are stars I like on the team, but also because it is a team full of miracles. In the 2021 playoffs, the Clippers reversed and won after losing two consecutive games at the beginning of two rounds. In addition, with Leonard injured, Paul George led the team to the Western Conference Finals, creating the best record in Clippers history. In the following season, despite Paul George and Kawhi Leonard missing most of the games, they still played eighth in the division and completed a 35 point comeback. Such a team will always give fans hope, let fans see the passion of the team, and make fans also full of passion.
  • Shanxi Team of CBA:The main reason why I like it is that it is a team from my hometown. I have visited the home stadium of Shanxi team several times to watch their basketball games, witnessing countless classic moments, their past lows, and their current strength. Although we are not always a strong team, Shanxi team has a tough playing style and never gives up. The fans are always passionate and love Shanxi team. The Shanxi team has a slogan based on dialects, which in Putonghua is "Shanxi Nao Ta". In this scene, it is a slogan for Shanxi fans to cheer for the Shanxi team and wish them a smooth victory over their opponents. Listening to the uniform slogans of the fans at home, watching Shanxi team win, and finally cheering for the victory with the fans, I can clearly feel my sense of honor as a Shanxi person and sincerely feel happy for Shanxi's victory.
